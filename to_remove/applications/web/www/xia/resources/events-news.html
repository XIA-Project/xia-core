3rd Bi-Annual XIA Meeting at CMU

The On November 10-11, 2011, the XIA Project held its third bi-annual project workshop on the CMU Campus. About 35-participated in the meeting. The first day focused on the reviewing results and lessons learned during the first year of the project. XIA Students and Faculty presented results in the areas of networking, security, economics and public policy and user studies. The first day also included a demo of XI over the GENI Testbed and a poster session. The second day was dedicated to planning for the coming year.

XIA Demo at GEC-12

The XIA project presented a demo of the XIA prototype at the 12th GENI Engineering Conference in Kansas City on November 2, 2011. The demo used 4-ProtoGENI nodes located in Los Angeles, Kansas City, Virginia and Texas and a node at CMU. The XIA Team used content retrieval, video streaming and live services to demonstrate various capabilities enabled by XIA, including the decoupling of application and network evolution, automatic service failover, and transitioning from IPv4 to XIP.




Second Bi-Annual XIA Project Meeting

The XIA Team held its second semi-annual project meeting at CMU on
May 16-17, 2011. The agenda included breakout sessions focusing on interactions between the four core research areas, a poster session at which students presented early research results, and plenary sessions on future applications and directions for XIA.


Kickoff Meeting

The XIA team held a kick off meeting for the project at CMU on

October 28-29. The goal for the meeting was to work out the research agenda for the XIA project, focusing on the interactions between the various research trusts. The meetings started with presentations on the status and vision for the four focus areas: core networking (see picture below), trust management, users, and public policy.
