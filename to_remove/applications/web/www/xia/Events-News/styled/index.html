XIA Kickoff Meeting

The XIA team held a kick off meeting for the project at CMU on October 28-29. The goal for the meeting was to work out the research agenda for the XIA project, focusing on the interactions between the various research trusts. The meetings started with presentations on the status and vision for the four focus areas: core networking (see picture below), trust management, users, and public policy.
