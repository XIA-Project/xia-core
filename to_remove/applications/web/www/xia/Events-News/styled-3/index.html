Third Bi-Annual XIA Meeting at CMU

On November 10-11, 2011, the XIA Project held its third bi-annual project workshop on the CMU Campus. About 35-participated in the meeting. The first day focused on the reviewing results and lessons learned during the first year of the project. XIA Students and Faculty presented results in the areas of networking, security, economics and public policy and user studies. The first day also included a demo of XIA over the GENI Testbed and a poster session. The second day was dedicated to planning for the coming year.
